Baird and Cisco to Host Tech Talk on Cisco's Multi-Cloud Strategy
SAN JOSE, CA--(Marketwired - Nov 28, 2017) - Cisco (NASDAQ: CSCO) will host a conference call with Baird and Cisco's Kip Compton, VP, Cloud Platform and Solutions to discuss the Company's cloud strategy to enable a multi-cloud world. Interested parties can find more information on Cisco's Investor Relations website at https://investor.cisco.com.
