While the stock had been oversold going into the earnings report having the company https://www.forbes.com/sites/chuckjones/2017/11/01/surprise-surprise-surprise-tesla-misses-guidance-again/#31ffc909304c target=_self>report a worse than expected loss and more important pushing out the Model 3 5,000 per week and 10,000 per week production goals it has fallen further into oversold territory. The shares have also reached a critical support level that traces back to April this year (see chart below).
Next support level is 20% lower
